THE WATCHER







INSTALLATION

In an era where information is king, the prevalence of fake news and social polarization threatens to distort our points of view. We can’t help but ask ourselves: what is the truth? By looking through the binoculars, my subject defines her own perspective amidst an array of “eyes” that represent conflicting opinions within a society. She strains to find clarity amidst the intrusion around her.


Size: 9’x16’x11’
Medium mixed media, acrylic paint, cardboard, twine, wood skewers, wood blocks
